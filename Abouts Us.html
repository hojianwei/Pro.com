We Specialise In


Repairs:

As well as knowledge in computer parts, new computer sales and computer support, That Computer Shop Staff have many years' experience in computer hardware and software repairs.

We can service all types of computers such as Acer, Dell, HP, Compaq, Toshiba, NEC, ASUS, Custom Systems etc.

Some of the symptoms of an unhealthy computer are the following:

  • Computer takes a long time to boot up 
  • Opening some applications takes forever 
  • The internet is unusually slow 
  • You often get annoying pop-up windows when on the internet 
  • Movies, Games and Images take a long time to open and are jumpy (screen-stutter) 
  • There are scraping or buzzing noises coming from the computer case 

If you're experiencing any of the above, then your computer needs an inspection by one of our highly skilled technicians to isolate the problem and fix it. It could be a simple fix that saves your precious photos, hours of frustration and potentially unsafe/unsecure internet browsing.

When it comes to important information, you can never be too safe - so speak with your local That Computer Shop today!
